BYNDWallet


BYNDWallet is the best standalone Beyondcoin wallet built for iOS and Android. It is available to download for free on the Apple App Store and Play Store!

BYNDWallet is...

Simple

BYNDWallet has a simplified codebase making it quick and lightweight, whilst still providing a responsive and powerful performance.

Secure

BYNDWallet uses AES hardware encryption and app sandboxing aswell as keychain and code signatures to protect your coins from malware and browser flaws.

Safe

BYNDWallet is deterministic so you can access your account using just a simple backup phrase on any device running the app if you happen to lose yours.

Quick

BYNDWallet connects directly to the Litecoin network to provide a powerful performance using ‘Simple Payment Verification’ (SPV Mode).

Sleek

BYNDWallet adapts the best design elements and features from existing wallets along with our own improvements and design choices.

Modern

BYNDWallet is feature rich and offers an in built price ticker making it the perfect all in one wallet when you're on the go.

Open

BYNDWallets code is freely available to check, review and port into your own projects on GitHub, so get building!

Ready

BYNDWallet doesnt have a server that can be hacked or go offline so you can always access your money anytime and anywhere.

Supportive

BYNDWallet can import your current Beyondcoin wallet quickly and easily so you can take your existing wallet with you.
